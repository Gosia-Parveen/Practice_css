MARSHMALLOW RECIPE


Ingredients

3 packets unflavored gelatin
            1 cup cold water (divided)    
            1½ cups granulated sugar    
            1 cup light corn syrup    
            ¼ tsp salt    
            1 tsp vanilla extract    
            Powdered sugar (for dusting)

Method

  • Bloom the gelatin:
    Add gelatin to ½ cup cold water in a large bowl. Let it sit for 10 minutes.
  • Make sugar syrup:
    In a saucepan, combine sugar, corn syrup, salt, and remaining ½ cup water.
    Heat on medium, stirring until sugar dissolves. Let it boil (do not stir) until it reaches soft-ball stage or about 115°C (240°F).
  • Whip it up:
    Slowly pour the hot syrup into the gelatin while beating with a hand or stand mixer on low.
    Increase speed to high and beat for 8–10 minutes until thick, fluffy, and glossy.
  • Flavor:
    Add vanilla extract and mix briefly.
  • Set the marshmallows:
    Pour mixture into a greased tray dusted with powdered sugar. Smooth the top and dust again.
    Let it set for 4–6 hours or overnight.
  • Cut & coat:
    Cut into cubes and toss in powdered sugar to prevent sticking.
